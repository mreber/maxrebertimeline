What is Scorched Earth?

In a literaly sense, the Scorched Earth is a region that once thrived being reduced to a state of barrenness, having lost the capacity for regeneration.
-
In a capitalistic sense, the Scorched Earth destroys groups of people, it destroys the methods in which communities pursue modes of self-sufficient subsistence, of self-governance or of mutual support.
-
The Scorched Earth is our future (arguably our present), the world in decay amongst a dystopic internet landscape.
-
The Scorched Earth is the stifiling of hope. The disempowerment of youth, the assault on the young. The internet complex being a means to prevent youth from experiencing and knowing itself.
-
The Scorched Earth reveals environmental realities that have been veiled behind the intangibility of wireless setups and systems, the placelessness of data, the illusions of the "virtual", the "cloud." The world vanquishing extremes of the "digital age."
-
The Scorched earth is the human analyzed, every sense, the ticks in the human eye, the frequencies of our breathing. Our humanness as a signal intercepted and commodified.
-
The Scorched earth is the fragmentation of a Lifeworld
-
