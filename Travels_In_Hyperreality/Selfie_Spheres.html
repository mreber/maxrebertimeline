"Selfies are an illustration of the precarious balance between using a new technology and being used by it." - Claudia Hart
-
My selfies are made through various layers of photo apps, 3D softwares & video softwares in order to tell a story of dysmorphia I've experienced on social platforms.
-
By working with digital simulations, dynamic systems, and displacement maps I aimed to create a grotesque and tangible image. I aimed to connect the viewer with visions of myself through digital applications, hoping that others would relate to the distorted realities and fantastical narratives these bodies were placed in.
