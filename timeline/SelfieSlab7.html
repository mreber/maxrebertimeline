Selfie Scroll

Selfie Scroll is a digital artwork composed of selfies found on my social media profile. Materializing the imagery in 3D software allows the images to engage with their own personal boundary in simulated reality. The selfie becomes embodied. When we view images with a feeling of digital tangibility, a tension is generated. The Selfie becomes fragile; images recoil and become distorted, their physicality in the process of becoming but not yet fully realized. This artwork asks us to think about our relationship with images of the human, where these images belong, and how these images can exhibit their own autonomy.
